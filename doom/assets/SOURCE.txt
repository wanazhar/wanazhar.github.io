DOOM shareware bundle source
============================

This directory includes doom.jsdos built from the publicly redistributable DOOM v1.9 shareware archive:

  Source mirror: https://www.gamers.org/pub/idgames/idstuff/doom/doom19s.zip
  Original archive: doom19s.zip
  SHA-256: cacf0142b31ca1af00796b4a0339e07992ac5f21bc3f81e7532fe1b5e1b486e6

The archive contains DOOM1.WAD, not the commercial DOOM.WAD.

Bundle config:
  - Based on the uploaded v3 iPad/Safari-safe dosbox.safe.conf
  - DOOM.EXE -nomusic
  - core=normal
  - cycles=fixed 20000
  - machine=vgaonly
  - ems=false
